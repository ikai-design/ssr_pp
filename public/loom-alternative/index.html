FAQ

Migrating from Loom

Answering developer and designer questions about switching your recording workflow.

Why is Simple Screen Recorder free?

Simple Screen Recorder is free because we compile and process everything on your client machine (no server cost). The planned $5/mo Pro tier will cover our hosting and database costs when sharing cloud video links.

How do I save and share my videos?

When you finish recording, you are taken to a local editor tab. Click "Export" to compile an MP4 directly to your Downloads folder. You can upload the MP4 anywhere: Slack, Notion, Jira, or email.

Does it support audio recording?

Yes. It automatically records system/tab audio and allows you to toggle your microphone on or off in the overlay before starting your presentation.

Is there a watermark?

No. We do not overlay watermarks, logo stamps, or timing metrics. The videos belong 100% to you and are ready for professional presentations.
