Why choose an extension over a desktop app?

Extensions run in sandboxed memory containers, protecting your desktop systems from heavy binary installs. Simple Screen Recorder launches instantly without dragging down CPU threads.

Is there a time limit on the free version?

No. Simple Screen Recorder doesn't impose time limitations, allowing you to capture long demos and tutorials with zero pricing triggers.
